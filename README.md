# Path To Zero
Calculate your individual carbon footprint, receive weekly snapshots, and work on your impact to a greener future!

Click Sign Up to create your personalized profile and spend 5 minutes every day answering a simple quiz to calculate your carbon footprint for the week. Visualize your impact with a graph and follow the tips and facts on our website to learn what small steps you could take to minimize your footprint. **Every Step Matters!**
